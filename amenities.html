Dining

CSCC offers breakfast, lunch and dinner. Our traditional menus are updated regularly and feature seasonal entrees and delicious weekly specials, carefully created by our Executive Chef.

In addition to our main Dining Room, our Members may dine in the Lounge which provides a casual setting to relax with friends and family. During the warmer months, we open our covered Patio with beautiful pool and course views. Private, well-appointed banquet rooms are also available for various functions.

Our Golf Patio overlooks the 9th green, and is a fun place to enjoy refreshments from the Founder's Grill following a round. Members and their guests at the Aquatic Center may order from our full dining menu or from the Founder's Grill.

Fitness

The Fitness Center is open every day and provides an assortment of equipment including treadmills, stationary bikes and ellipticals for cardio workouts, as well as hand held weights and resistance equipment for strength training.

Complimentary group classes include Circuit Training, Boot Camp, Zumba, Yoga, Mat Pilates and specialty Workshops. Personal fitness assessments and customized programs are offered by Personal Trainers when possible.

Aquatics

The recently renovated Aquatics Complex at Colorado Springs Country Club provides a full program of lessons, safety and fun for every member of the family. Centering the complex is our child-friendly large pool, featuring a slide and diving board. In addition, there is a shallow-water toddler pool and a four-lane lap pool. Lifeguards are certified in CPR, First Aid and Lifeguarding. The pools are situated within a beautifully landscaped fenced area, and conveniently located between the Founder's Grill and Clubhouse.

We provide aquatic programing including:

  • Water Aerobics classes
  • Swimming lessons
  • Pool-centered activities throughout the summer, such as Dive-In Movies and Holiday celebrations for Memorial Day, Father's Day, 4th of July and Labor Day
  • Colorado Springs Country Club Swim Team – a local competitive team

Kate Doane, manager of the CSCC Aquatics Program, is a graduate of Indiana University with a B.S. in Kinesiology and an M.S. in Health Science. Kate played varsity water polo for IU and was named an All-American in her sport. In addition to coaching the Colorado Springs Swim Team, Kate is head coach for both the Cheyenne Mountain High School Girls and Boys swim teams. In 2022, she was awarded Boys Swim and Dive 4A CHSAA Coach of the Year again. She is also a certified instructor for CPR, First Aid, and Lifeguard training.

Racket Sports

Tennis

Colorado Springs Country Club offers a variety of tennis programs for adults and kids. Our four outdoor courts are open year-round, and two have lighting until 10 p.m.

CSCC offers adult lessons including group, semi-private, and private. We have a Master’s Tennis program, as well as competitive USTA women’s and mixed teams. Additionally, complimentary beginning tennis lessons and Fun Days are available.

Our youth programs promote fun, exercise, and skill development. We offer tennis lessons for children aged three and up. We also have a competitive USTA Junior Team that plays other clubs in the area.

Pickleball

CSCC offers complimentary classes and sessions for all levels, social events, member open play, as well as private lessons with our Director and staff. Courts can be reserved on the web site for your preferred time and group size.

Social

Social activities are held throughout the year, and inclusive of all ages. These are enjoyed by our small, friendly community of members, and include:

  • Holiday and cocktail parties, live music by the pool, wine pairing dinners, our traditional Chicken & Bingo, Pickleball Socials, and Trivia Nights
  • Mahjong and Bridge groups
  • Book and Craft clubs
  • Family-friendly Halloween, Easter Bunny and Santa parties, movie and game nights
  • Outstanding menus that are carefully created by the Club’s Executive Chef and Pastry Chef

Caddy Shack

Our Super Awesome Kids' Recreation Center!

Stocked with art supplies, games, movies, and more, Caddy Shack is a place for kids to be kids. Well-equipped with toys, stuffed animals, and games, kids have a wide variety of activities to choose from. Maybe your child would like to check out our big screen room with comfy bean-bags for watching movies or playing our Wii. Or kids can play a variety of video games on our PlayStation 4 or Xbox One systems. Our air-hockey table is a popular spot for friendly competition. Drawing, coloring, and writing on our chalkboard wall are also fun options.
