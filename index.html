Who are we
Colorado Springs Country Club
Real People. Unreal Golf.

Welcome to Colorado Springs Country Club. We are member-owned, with a history of bringing friends and family together. Our private Club is centrally located, yet situated on over 100 pristine acres with sweeping views of the beautiful Rocky Mountains and Palmer Park.

More than just a private golf club, CSCC features top amenities including a newly renovated aquatics complex, fitness center and classes, fine dining, social events, and tennis and pickleball.

The Clubhouse foundation is a beautiful, natural rock barn built in 1918. This historical building is the center for many of our gatherings and parties. Tastefully renovated and traditionally appointed, the ambiance is always inviting.
