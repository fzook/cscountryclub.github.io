Membership Options
Designed to meet your Needs

Whether you're looking to imrove your fittness, relax on the golf course, or spend more quality time with family, the Colorado Springs Country Club offers a wide range of membership options to meet your needs. Become a member today and begin your experience.

All applications for membership are subject to approval by the Club's Board of Directors.

  • Resident Membership
    Resident Memberships provide full Club privileges, including access to unlimited golf, fitness center and classes, tennis and pickleball courts, a newly renovated aquatics center, and dining and social events. Resident memberships are available for both singles and families.
  • Junior Executive Memberships
    Junior Executive Memberships offer full Club privileges as listed under Resident membership. Ages 21 - 39. Available for both singles and families.
    The age requirement for Junior Executive I is 21-34.
    The age requirement for Junior Executive II is 35-39.
  • Non-Resident Membership
    Non-Resident Memberships offer full Club privileges. To be eligible, your principle place of business and residence must be located outside a 60-mile radius of the boundary of the Club's real property.
  • Social Membership
    The Social level offers two golf rounds each month (non prime time for $55) and optional access to practice facilities. The fitness center and classes, tennis and pickleball, aquatics, dining and social activities are included.
  • Recreational Membership
    Recreational Memberships provide access to fitness, tennis and pickleball, aquatics, dining and social activities.
  • Clubhouse Membership
    Clubhouse Memberships include access to social events and dining.
